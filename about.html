Who We Are

Breath2Help (B2H) is a humanitarian-first foundation built on ethics, responsibility, and long-term impact. Every contribution is directed toward sustainable relief systems designed to serve humanity beyond short-term aid.

“Technology should serve humanity without compromising ethics.”

— Mubashir Munnawar

Our Vision

A world where relief, dignity, education, and care continue regardless of economic conditions — through systems designed to serve generations to come.

Our Mission

To transform participation into sustainable humanitarian mechanisms that continuously serve humanity, creating true Sadaqah Jariyah for participants and beneficiaries alike.

Core Values

  • Integrity — principles over shortcuts
  • Trust — credibility over hype
  • Patience — sustainability over speed
  • Humanity — people over profit
  • Transparency — clarity within ethical boundaries

Impact: Hundreds of thousands of lives reached through ethical relief initiatives, long-term assistance programs, and humanitarian systems.

Founder’s Intention

Breath2Help was founded by Mubashir Munnawar with a single guiding principle: humanitarian systems must never serve personal gain. The mission is protected through ethical governance, patience, and accountability — for today and for generations to come.
