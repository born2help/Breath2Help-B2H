Who We Are

B2H (Burn to Help | Born for Humanity) is a humanitarian-first foundation created to ensure that helping people is not dependent on trends, emotions, or market cycles. We are built on ethics, responsibility, and long-term impact — not speculation.

Our Vision

A world where systems themselves are designed to help humanity — where relief, dignity, education, and care continue regardless of economic conditions or individual wealth.

Our Mission

To transform contributions into sustainable, self-generating mechanisms that continuously serve humanity, creating a true Sadaqah Jariyah for both participants and beneficiaries.

Founder’s Intention

B2H was founded by Mubashir Munnawar with a simple but powerful belief: technology should serve humanity without compromising ethics. There is no personal financial gain embedded in this foundation — every decision is made with patience, accountability, and responsibility before Allah.

Core Values

  • Integrity — over speed and shortcuts
  • Trust — over hype and noise
  • Patience — over pressure and instant results
  • Humanity — over profit and popularity
  • Transparency — within ethical and safe boundaries

Our Commitment

B2H exists to protect dignity and create sustainable relief for the most vulnerable. Every system, structure, and process is designed for long-term stewardship — ensuring that help continues not for years, but for generations.
