Who We Are

B2H (Burn to Help | Born for Humanity) is a humanitarian-first initiative that leverages technology to create long-lasting impact. We prioritize human welfare, ethical stewardship, and sustainability over speculation or financial hype.

Our Vision

A world where helping others is embedded into systems, creating continuous relief and opportunities for all, irrespective of wealth or geography.

Our Mission

To transform contributions into sustainable, self-generating systems that serve humanity continuously, creating a true Sadaqah Jariyah for participants and beneficiaries alike.

Founder’s Story

B2H was founded by Mubashir Munnawar, driven by a belief that technology can be structured to help humanity while remaining fully ethical, transparent, and lawful. With no personal financial gain involved, every decision is made with patience, responsibility, and integrity.

Core Values

  • Integrity: Over speed and convenience
  • Trust: Over hype and speculation
  • Patience: Over pressure and instant gratification
  • Humanity: Over profit and attention
  • Transparency: Within ethical and safe boundaries

Our Commitment

B2H exists to create sustainable impact for the poor and vulnerable. We are committed to transparency, ethical management, and long-term stewardship, ensuring that every participant contributes to real-world humanitarian outcomes.
