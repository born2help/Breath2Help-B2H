Who We Are

Breath2Help (B2H) is a humanitarian-first foundation built to protect dignity, reduce suffering, and create systems that continue serving long after we are gone. We are not built around trends, speculation, or personalities — but around responsibility, amanah (sacred trust), and long-term impact. Every contribution strengthens sustainable relief mechanisms designed to benefit humanity across generations.

“Humanitarian work must be protected from ego, profit, and temporary thinking. It must be built to serve long after its founder is gone.”

— Mubashir Munnawar

Our Vision

A world where relief, dignity, education, and essential care continue uninterrupted — protected by ethical systems that do not collapse with markets, leadership changes, or economic instability. A world where compassion is structured to last.

Our Mission

To transform participation into sustainable humanitarian infrastructure — where every contribution becomes part of a continuous cycle of impact, creating true Sadaqah Jariyah for those who give and lasting support for those in need.

Core Values

  • Integrity — principles before convenience
  • Trust — transparency over promises
  • Patience — sustainability over speed
  • Human Dignity — people before profit
  • Accountability — responsibility beyond recognition

Impact: Expanding humanitarian reach through ethical relief initiatives, long-term assistance programs, and systems designed to grow stronger with time. B2H measures success not by visibility — but by lives protected and dignity preserved.

Founder’s Intention

Breath2Help was founded by Mubashir Munnawar with one unwavering principle: humanitarian systems must never serve personal enrichment. The founder holds no ownership privilege, no extraction rights, and no financial entitlement. The role exists only to guard the mission, protect its ethics, and ensure it remains aligned with faith, accountability, and humanity — today and for generations to come.
