Who We Are

B2H (Burn to Help | Born for Humanity) is a humanitarian-first foundation. We convert every effort into measurable, sustainable aid. Ethics, responsibility, and long-term impact guide all our decisions.

"Technology should serve humanity without compromising ethics."
— Mubashir Munnawar

Our Vision

A world where relief, dignity, education, and care persist regardless of wealth or economic conditions. Systems exist not just for the present but for generations to come.

Our Mission

We transform contributions into sustainable mechanisms that continuously serve humanity, creating a true Sadaqah Jariyah for participants and beneficiaries alike.

Core Values

  • Integrity — over shortcuts or speed
  • Trust — over hype or trends
  • Patience — over instant gratification
  • Humanity — over profit or popularity
  • Transparency — within ethical and safe boundaries
Impact: Over 500,000 people reached this year through humanitarian programs.

Founder’s Intention

B2H was founded by Mubashir Munnawar with a single principle: technology should serve humanity without personal gain. Every decision is made with patience, accountability, and full ethical responsibility.
