Who We Are

B2H (Burn to Help | Born for Humanity) is a humanitarian-first foundation built on ethics, responsibility, and long-term impact. Every contribution is transformed into measurable, sustainable aid designed to serve humanity beyond short-term relief.

“Technology should serve humanity without compromising ethics.”

— Mubashir Munnawar

Our Vision

A world where relief, dignity, education, and care continue regardless of economic conditions — through systems designed to serve not just today, but generations to come.

Our Mission

To transform contributions into sustainable humanitarian mechanisms that continuously serve humanity, creating true Sadaqah Jariyah for both participants and beneficiaries.

Core Values

  • Integrity — principles over shortcuts
  • Trust — credibility over hype
  • Patience — sustainability over speed
  • Humanity — people over profit
  • Transparency — clarity within ethical boundaries

Impact: More than 500,000 people reached through humanitarian initiatives, ethical relief systems, and long-term assistance programs.

Founder’s Intention

B2H was founded by Mubashir Munnawar with a single guiding principle: technology must serve humanity without personal gain. Every decision is made with patience, accountability, and ethical responsibility, ensuring the mission remains protected for generations.
