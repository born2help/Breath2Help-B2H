Who Can Volunteer

B2H welcomes individuals of all ages and backgrounds who are committed to humanitarian work, ethical participation, and helping communities in need. Volunteering is voluntary, value-driven, and non-profit oriented.

Volunteer Principles

  • Service with integrity, patience, and dedication
  • Adherence to ethical guidelines and accountability
  • Respect for community and participants
  • Contribution to sustainable humanitarian impact

How It Works

Once you join, you will receive guidance from our volunteer coordinators. Opportunities include event support, online awareness campaigns, and community assistance. All tasks are aligned with B2H’s long-term mission to serve humanity responsibly.

Important Notice

Participation is voluntary. Volunteers are expected to adhere to B2H’s ethical and faith-aligned principles. No financial return, promises, or benefits are implied by volunteering.
