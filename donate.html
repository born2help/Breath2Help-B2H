Purpose of Donations

Donations to B2H directly support humanitarian initiatives, ethical operations, and long-term mission sustainability. Donations are not speculative, refundable, or profit-generating.

What Your Donation Supports

  • Humanitarian relief and emergency assistance
  • Education, healthcare, and food security programs
  • Operational sustainability of B2H
  • Compliance, protection, and responsible coordination

What Donations Are NOT

  • Not an investment vehicle
  • Not a promise of financial return
  • Not linked to token price or market activity
  • Not a speculative or trading instrument

Participation Principles

Donations are accepted only from individuals who understand and align with B2H’s humanitarian-first and ethical principles.

Sadaqah Jariyah

B2H enables participation as Sadaqah Jariyah — a form of continuous charity that benefits people over time, by the will of Allah.

Donation Methods

Approved donation methods will be publicly announced through official B2H channels only.

Any request for private, direct, or unofficial payments should be treated as misinformation.

Important Notice

Anyone promising guaranteed returns, special access, or financial benefits does not represent B2H. Always rely on official, ethical channels.
