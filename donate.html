Purpose of Donations

Donations to Breath2Help (B2H) directly support humanitarian initiatives, ethical operations, and long-term mission sustainability. Contributions are voluntary, fully transparent, and not refundable.

Every donation is a conscious act of care — ensuring relief reaches those who need it most.

How Your Donation Is Used

  • Humanitarian relief and emergency assistance
  • Education, healthcare, and food security programs
  • Operational and administrative sustainability
  • Compliance, accountability, and ethical oversight

We ensure every dollar or rupiah reaches its intended purpose, measured and reported transparently.

What Donations Are NOT

  • Not an investment
  • Not a promise of returns
  • Not speculative or tradeable

B2H exists to serve humanity, not profit or speculation. Donations strictly fund humanitarian efforts.
