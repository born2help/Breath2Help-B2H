Purpose of Donations

Donations to B2H directly support humanitarian initiatives, ethical operations, and long-term mission sustainability. Contributions are entirely voluntary and not refundable, speculative, or profit-generating.

How Your Donation Is Used

  • Humanitarian relief and emergency assistance
  • Education, healthcare, and food security programs
  • Operational and administrative sustainability of B2H
  • Compliance, accountability, and responsible program management

What Donations Are NOT

  • Not an investment vehicle
  • Not a promise of financial return or yield
  • Not linked to token price, market activity, or speculation
  • Not intended for trading or profit purposes

Participation Principles

Donations are accepted only from individuals who understand and align with B2H’s humanitarian-first, ethical, and faith-aligned principles.

Sadaqah Jariyah

B2H enables donations as Sadaqah Jariyah — a form of continuous charity that benefits people over time, by the will of Allah. Contributions generate ethical, sustainable, and long-term impact.
