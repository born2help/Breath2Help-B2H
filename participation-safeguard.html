Principles of Participation

B2H participation is voluntary, ethical, and guided by humanitarian principles.

  • No coercion — every participant chooses freely
  • Clear ethical boundaries protect all stakeholders
  • Participation never compromises dignity, trust, or mission values

Safeguards in Action

B2H implements layered safeguards to prevent misuse, exploitation, or undue influence:

  • Founder authority is limited to ethical oversight only
  • Financial and operational transparency within ethical limits
  • Checks to prevent reckless or manipulative actions

Participant Protections

Every contributor is protected from risks that do not align with mission values:

  • Ethical engagement rules prevent loss from speculation
  • Education and clear communication on participation risks
  • Continuous oversight ensures accountability and trust

Core Commitment

Participation is always a pathway to helping humanity, not personal gain. Every action is safeguarded ethically and transparently.
