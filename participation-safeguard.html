Burn to Help — What It Means

In B2H, burning is not destruction. It is a conscious act of responsibility.

Every burn exists to protect dignity, reduce suffering, and sustain the mission.

Unlike other projects that burn for hype, B2H burns create real-world impact, stability, and relief.

How Burns Serve Humanity

A portion of what is sacrificed is directed toward humanitarian purpose, operational sustainability, and continued service.

Helping people is an obligation, not a marketing strategy.

Protection for Participants

B2H designs burns with balance: never reckless, never emotional, never driven by short-term pressure.

The goal is sustainability so helping others does not sacrifice trust.

Faith, Accountability & Permanence

Every burn is carried out with accountability, transparency, and respect for human dignity.

Ethics in B2H

All actions are guided by responsibility, transparency, and fairness.

Every contribution serves humanity only. Exploitation or personal enrichment is rejected.

Core Ethical Principles

  • Transparency in all funds and actions
  • Accountability for every decision
  • Protection of dignity over profit
  • Voluntary participation and informed consent

Founder Safeguards

The Founder holds no ownership or financial privilege. Authority exists only to safeguard values and prevent misuse.

Transparency rules, financial locks, audit pathways, and mission-freeze mechanisms ensure no single individual can override purpose.
