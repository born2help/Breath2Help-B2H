Amanah, Not Power

B2H was established as an amanah (trust), not as a transferable position of power. Leadership exists only to safeguard the mission — never to dominate or monetize it.

This framework ensures continuity, protection against capture, and immunity from greed-driven removal.

Founder Protection Clause

The founder of B2H cannot be removed, overridden, suspended, or sidelined by any individual, committee, vote, investor group, or external authority as long as he remains:

  • Mentally capable
  • Ethically aligned with the Constitution
  • Actively safeguarding the mission

This protection exists to prevent hostile takeovers, internal coups, popularity-based displacement, or capital-driven interference.

No Ownership — Only Guardianship

The founder does not own B2H. He is its first guardian.

Authority is not a reward or entitlement. It is a responsibility that exists only to protect people and purpose.

Limits on Founder Authority

Founder protection does not mean unlimited power.

  • No redirection of mission for personal gain
  • No extraction of wealth or luxury
  • No suppression of ethical accountability
  • No override of the Constitution

Protection exists to guard purpose — not to enable abuse.

Succession Through Trust — Not Ambition

In the event of age, illness, or reduced physical capacity, authority does not transfer automatically, publicly, or competitively.

Succession occurs only through private, lifetime designation by the founder.

Designated Custodians

Custodians are selected based on:

  • Proven character
  • Long-term restraint
  • Resistance to greed
  • Alignment with humanitarian purpose

No custodian gains ownership, permanent authority, or personal entitlement.

Absolute Rejection of External Control

No external body — including governments, investors, donors, committees, or boards — may remove the founder or override succession designations.

Compliance occurs only where it does not violate conscience, mission, or humanitarian priority.

Emergency Continuity Clause

In the event of sudden death or irreversible incapacity, authority transfers immediately to pre-designated custodians.

No interim voting, emergency board, or temporary control body is permitted.

Final Ethical Anchor

In Islamic accountability, removing a trustworthy guardian without ethical cause is a violation of amanah.

Therefore, leadership continuity in B2H is governed by conscience, constitution, and responsibility before Allah — not by power, popularity, or capital.
