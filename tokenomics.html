Token Overview

  • Token Name: B2H
  • Meaning: Burn to Help · Born for Humanity
  • Total Supply: 500,000,000,000 B2H
  • Supply Model: Fixed — No future minting

Fixed Supply Principle

B2H has a permanently fixed supply. No additional tokens can ever be created. This design prevents dilution, ensures predictability, and supports long-term trust for humanitarian stewardship.

Burn-to-Help Mechanism

The Burn-to-Help mechanism permanently removes tokens from circulation while activating predefined allocations toward humanitarian initiatives and project sustainability.

Token burns are not intended to influence market price or guarantee value. Their sole purpose is to align participation with real-world impact.

Allocation Philosophy

  • 5% — Humanitarian relief and aid programs
  • 3% — Operational sustainability and infrastructure
  • Security, compliance, and ethical governance
  • Minimal and responsible administration

What Tokenomics Do NOT Promise

  • No guaranteed returns
  • No profit-sharing claims
  • No price appreciation targets
  • No speculative incentives

Participation Ethics

Participation in B2H should be based on alignment with humanitarian values and ethical intent — not financial expectations or trading behavior.

Founder Stewardship

Tokenomic decisions are guided by responsibility and long-term mission protection. The founder retains stewardship authority solely to safeguard the humanitarian vision, never for personal financial extraction.

Risk Disclosure

Participation involves risk. B2H does not guarantee financial outcomes, returns, or asset appreciation. Individuals should engage only with full understanding of its humanitarian-first nature.
