Preamble

Breath2Help (B2H) exists as a humanitarian-first foundation, rooted in ethical responsibility, faith-aligned principles, and long-term stewardship. This Constitution ensures that participation, leadership, and operations are guided by trust, accountability, and purpose.

Core Principles

  • Ethical Responsibility — human dignity always comes first
  • Transparency — honest reporting within safe boundaries
  • Faith Alignment — no Riba, exploitation, or unjust enrichment
  • Stewardship — authority exists only to protect the mission
  • Continuity — leadership guided by trust, not popularity

Participant Rights & Safeguards

Participation is voluntary and fully protected. Participants have the right to:

  • Receive accurate, ethical, and safe information
  • Be protected from misuse or exploitation
  • Understand transparency limits applied for safety
  • Access clear humanitarian impact reporting

Leadership & Founder Protection

The Founder’s authority exists solely to safeguard B2H’s mission and values. Protection mechanisms prevent:

  • External manipulation or hostile takeovers
  • Capital-driven or popularity-based removal
  • Misuse of authority for personal gain

Leadership remains accountable, ethically bound, and mission-locked.

Burn-to-Help Governance

All burns are governed by strict humanitarian oversight:

  • Direct, measurable humanitarian relief impact
  • Operational sustainability and efficiency
  • Long-term trust, stability, and ethical alignment

Financial & Ethical Boundaries

  • No speculative promises or profit guarantees
  • No dividends, luxury extraction, or personal enrichment
  • Auditable systems maintained within ethical limits

Financial discipline ensures purpose is never compromised.

Final Ethical Commitment

Every contributor, custodian, and participant upholds this Constitution. Decisions are guided by conscience, ethical duty, and humanitarian impact. Humanity, faith, and trust remain above all else.
