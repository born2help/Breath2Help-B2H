Constitution of B2H Global Foundation

Guided by Ethics · Built for Humanity · Protected by Trust

← Back Home Transparency Founder Continuity

Preamble

B2H (Burn to Help · Born for Humanity) exists as a humanitarian instrument, rooted in ethical responsibility, faith-aligned principles, and long-term stewardship. This Constitution governs participation, leadership, and operations to ensure purpose and trust are never compromised.

Core Principles

  • Ethical Responsibility: Every action prioritizes human dignity and long-term benefit.
  • Transparency: Clear reporting within ethical and safety boundaries.
  • Faith Alignment: No Riba, exploitation, or unjust enrichment.
  • Stewardship: Leadership exists to protect mission, not personal gain.
  • Continuity: Founder and custodianship governed by trust and ethical selection.

Participant Rights & Safeguards

All participants engage voluntarily and have the right to:

  • Receive accurate, safe, and ethical information
  • Be protected from misuse, harm, or exploitation
  • Understand limits of transparency for safety
  • Access reports on humanitarian outcomes

Leadership & Founder Protection

The Founder holds authority only to uphold B2H’s mission. Protection clauses prevent:

  • Hostile takeovers or external interference
  • Popularity or capital-driven removal
  • Misuse of authority for personal gain

Leadership is accountable, transparent, and bound by ethical review.

Burn-to-Help Governance

All token burns follow strict guidelines to:

  • Direct impact toward humanitarian relief
  • Ensure operational continuity
  • Protect participant trust and system stability

Financial & Ethical Boundaries

  • No speculative promises, profit-sharing, or dividends
  • No unauthorized extraction or luxury allocation
  • Auditable operations within ethical limits

Accountability ensures the mission is never compromised for financial gain.

Final Ethical Commitment

All members, contributors, and custodians uphold the B2H Constitution. Decisions are guided by conscience, ethical duty, and humanitarian impact. The mission, people, and purpose are prioritized above all.
