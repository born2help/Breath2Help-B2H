Preamble

B2H (Burn to Help · Born for Humanity) exists as a humanitarian instrument, rooted in ethical responsibility, faith-aligned principles, and long-term stewardship. This Constitution governs participation, leadership, and operations to ensure purpose and trust are never compromised.

Core Principles

  • Ethical Responsibility — human dignity always comes first
  • Transparency — honest reporting within safety boundaries
  • Faith Alignment — no Riba, exploitation, or unjust enrichment
  • Stewardship — authority exists only to protect mission
  • Continuity — leadership governed by trust, not popularity

Participant Rights & Safeguards

All participation is voluntary and protected. Participants have the right to:

  • Accurate, ethical, and safe information
  • Protection from misuse or exploitation
  • Understanding transparency limits for safety
  • Access to humanitarian impact reporting

Leadership & Founder Protection

The Founder holds authority only to safeguard the mission and values of B2H. Protection mechanisms prevent:

  • Hostile takeovers or external manipulation
  • Capital-driven or popularity-based removal
  • Misuse of authority for personal gain

Leadership remains accountable, ethically bound, and mission-locked.

Burn-to-Help Governance

All burns follow strict humanitarian governance:

  • Direct humanitarian relief impact
  • Operational sustainability
  • Long-term trust and system stability

Financial & Ethical Boundaries

  • No speculative promises or profit guarantees
  • No dividends, luxury extraction, or enrichment
  • Auditable systems within ethical limits

Financial discipline ensures purpose is never compromised.

Final Ethical Commitment

All contributors, custodians, and participants uphold this Constitution. Decisions are guided by conscience, ethical duty, and humanitarian impact. Humanity, faith, and trust stand above all else.
