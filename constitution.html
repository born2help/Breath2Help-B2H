Purpose of the Constitution

The B2H Constitution defines the rules, ethical boundaries, and operational safeguards to ensure every action serves humanity, preserves dignity, and aligns with faith-based principles.

Core Principles

  • Humanitarian Intent — All contributions serve the needy and vulnerable
  • Ethical Governance — No exploitation, interest (Riba), or personal enrichment
  • Transparency Within Limits — Protect people first, exposure second
  • Accountability — Founders, trustees, and contributors follow strict oversight
  • Longevity — Systems designed to outlive individuals

Participation Safeguards

Contributors participate voluntarily and with full understanding. B2H ensures no participant is exposed to undue risk.

Stress Testing & Stability

All processes, token mechanisms, and humanitarian allocations are stress-tested to maintain stability, trust, and continuous service under varying scenarios.

Vision & Mission Alignment

The Constitution ensures every action, burn, and contribution aligns with B2H's long-term vision and mission.

Founder Authority & Safeguards

The Founder has stewardship authority only to protect the mission. Financial locks, audits, and ethical pathways prevent any misuse or override of the Constitution.
