Donation & Ethical Investment Model

B2H operates on a faith-inspired, asset-backed humanitarian model. Contributions are structured to ensure long-term impact, transparency, and continuous benefit for the poor and needy worldwide.

Donations (Sadqa-e-Jariyah)

All donation funds are utilized exclusively to acquire or develop long-term assets such as land, facilities, or income-generating projects. These assets are never sold. Only the profits generated are used continuously to support the poor, making every donation a Sadqa-e-Jariyah for the donor.

Ethical Investment Participation

Investors may participate separately from donations. Profits generated from investment-backed projects are shared on a transparent 70% (Investor) and 30% (Foundation) basis. The principal assets remain permanently owned by B2H to ensure sustainability.

Asset Protection & Permanence

All assets acquired through donations or investments remain protected under the foundation. Only profits may be distributed ‚Äî assets are preserved to secure long-term humanitarian benefit for future generations.

Founder-Led Mission Governance

To protect the original humanitarian mission, B2H follows a founder-led governance structure. Strategic and ethical decisions are made to preserve the foundation‚Äôs vision, integrity, and long-term commitment to humanity.
