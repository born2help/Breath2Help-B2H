Donation & Investment Model

Every contribution to B2H creates permanent impact. Assets remain forever ‚Äî only benefits are distributed.

üíö Donation Model (Sadaqah-e-Jariyah)

All donations received by B2H are invested strictly into long-term assets such as land, infrastructure, and income-generating projects.

These assets are never sold. Only the profit generated is used continuously to support the poor, education, healthcare, food programs, and emergency relief.

Every donor receives ongoing reward (Sadaqah-e-Jariyah) as long as the asset continues to generate benefit.

üìà Investment Model

Investors may participate by funding approved projects managed by B2H. The invested capital remains permanently as an asset of the foundation.

Profit generated from the investment is shared as follows:

  • 70% to the Investor
  • 30% to B2H Foundation (for social welfare)

Only profits are distributed ‚Äî capital remains protected and intact.
