Message from the Founder

"The poorest person on earth has something to give. B2H exists to prove it — and to make sure their giving reaches someone who needs it today."

My name is Mubashir Munnawar. I am 57 years old, and I built Breath2Help (B2H) with my own hands, my own family, and a single conviction that has never left me: no one should die feeling that their life made no difference.

B2H was born from a question that kept me awake at night — what about the person who is too poor to donate? What about the man or woman who looks at the suffering around them and feels helpless, because they have nothing to give? I refused to accept that they must remain on the sidelines of humanity's greatest work.

So I designed B2H around them. A person who buys a single B2H token worth one cent — without even fully understanding what they have done — automatically becomes part of feeding someone hungry, educating a child, or supporting a family in crisis. They are no longer helpless. They are helpers. That transformation of dignity is the entire soul of this project.

I am not a billionaire. I did not build this with vast resources. I built it with purpose, with the support of my family whom I trust completely, and through a legally registered foundation in Jakarta, Indonesia — because I wanted this to be real, verifiable, and built to last beyond my own lifetime.

I hold no ownership of B2H. I take no personal profit. Every token, every donation, and every transaction flows through a transparent, ethical system — because the moment this mission serves one person's ego or wallet, it has failed. This is Sadaqah Jariyah. A continuous charity. It must remain pure.

At 57, I understand something deeply: wealth stays in the world when you leave it, but a good deed travels with you. B2H is my promise — to the poor, to the forgotten, and to God — that as long as I breathe, this mission will breathe with me. And long after I am gone, it will breathe still.

You do not need to be rich to walk this path with me. You only need to care. Because in the end, the greatest return any of us will ever earn is not measured in money — it is measured in the lives we touched, and the dignity we restored.

— Mubashir Munnawar, Founder & Guardian of Vision, Breath2Help Global Foundation, Jakarta, Indonesia
