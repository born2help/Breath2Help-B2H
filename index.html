Governance

B2H governance is built on transparency, accountability, and community trust. Major decisions related to fund allocation, partnerships, and ecosystem development are guided by a structured governance framework. Community voices, advisory boards, and ethical oversight ensure that all actions remain aligned with humanitarian values. Governance at B2H is designed to prevent misuse, ensure clarity, and protect the long-term vision of the foundation.

Constitution

The B2H Constitution defines the ethical, operational, and humanitarian principles that guide the foundation. It establishes clear rules for charity distribution, burn mechanisms, financial transparency, and decision-making authority. This constitution ensures that all operations remain fair, auditable, and aligned with the core mission of serving humanity without compromise.

Why B2H?

Born to Help (B2H) was created with a clear humanitarian purpose — to merge blockchain innovation with real-world impact. B2H is not just a token, but a movement designed to support charity, transparency, and sustainable aid systems. Through structured governance, responsible tokenomics, and community participation, B2H ensures that every contribution creates measurable benefit for humanity. Our mission is simple: technology with conscience.
