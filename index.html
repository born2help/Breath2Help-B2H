Message from the Founder

"The poorest person on earth has something to give. B2H exists to prove it — and to make sure their giving reaches someone who needs it today."

I am Mubashir Munnawar, 57 years old. I built Breath2Help (B2H) with my own hands, my family by my side, and a single belief: no one should die feeling their life made no difference.

B2H was born from a simple, yet powerful question: what about those too poor to donate, those who feel helpless in the face of suffering? I refused to let them remain on the sidelines.

Here, even the smallest act — buying a single B2H token worth one cent — transforms a life. A hungry child fed, a family supported, a struggling soul lifted. Suddenly, the powerless become helpers. That dignity, that spark of humanity, is the heart of B2H.

I am not a billionaire. I built this with purpose, transparency, and love. I take no profit. Every token, every donation, flows through a system built to last, beyond ego and beyond me. This is Sadaqah Jariyah — continuous charity, pure and eternal.

At 57, I know this truth: wealth stays in the world, but good deeds travel with you. B2H is my promise — to the poor, the forgotten, and to God — that as long as I breathe, this mission will breathe. And long after I am gone, it will continue.

You don’t need riches to join. You only need a heart that cares. Because the greatest return we can earn is not money — it is the lives we touch and the dignity we restore.

— Mubashir Munnawar, Founder & Guardian of Vision, Breath2Help Global Foundation, Jakarta, Indonesia

Foundational Constitution

Purpose Above All: B2H exists for one reason — to reduce suffering, protect human dignity, and stand beside those who have no one to stand for them. Our mission is permanent and will never be changed for profit, power, or personal gain.

Faith & Humanity First: B2H is built on compassion, justice, amanah (sacred trust), and the intention of sadaqah jariyah — a charity that continues to benefit others for generations. Every decision is guided by conscience before convenience.

Transparency & Trust: Every contribution is treated as a sacred trust. We believe people deserve clarity, honesty, and proof of impact — not promises. View our Transparency Framework.

Protected Humanitarian Allocation: A fixed portion of every transaction is permanently dedicated to direct humanitarian aid, long-term sustainability, and structural protection. This commitment cannot be reduced or redirected away from those in need.

No Riba / No Exploitation: All financial activity follows ethical, interest-free principles. B2H will never be built on debt-based systems, manipulation, or unfair gain.

Founder as Guardian — Not Owner: The founder holds no ownership, no dividend rights, and receives no personal financial benefit. The role exists only to protect the mission and ensure it never drifts away from serving humanity.

Designed to Outlive Us: B2H is structured to continue beyond any one person. Clear succession and protection mechanisms ensure the mission remains intact for future generations.

Structural Safeguards: Built-in protections — including transparency controls and mission locks — permanently guard B2H from misuse, takeover, or mission drift.
