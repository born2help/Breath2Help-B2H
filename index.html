Governance, Constitution & Trustees

B2H Global Foundation is governed by a constitution that places purpose above power, transparency above control, and humanity above all interests.

Foundational Constitution

  • Purpose Above All: B2H exists solely to reduce suffering and protect human dignity.
  • Transparency & Trust: Every contribution is a sacred trust.
  • No Riba / Interest: All financial activity follows ethical and faith-aligned principles.
  • Founder as Guardian: No ownership, no personal benefit, no financial privilege.
  • Ethical Oversight: Rotating council ensures accountability.
  • Structural Safeguards: Audit locks, mission-freeze, and transparency rules.

Donations & Real Assets

Direct donations to B2H are not consumed and forgotten. They are invested into real assets such as real estate and essential infrastructure.

  • Remain permanently owned by the B2H Foundation
  • Can never be sold, seized, or acquired
  • Generate halal income
  • Send profits directly to humanitarian relief

As long as B2H exists, these assets serve humanity — creating a modern, transparent form of Sadaqah Jariyah.
