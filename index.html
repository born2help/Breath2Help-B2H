About B2H

B2H (Burn to Help | Born for Humanity) was created to transform blockchain into a tool for mercy. Unlike purely speculative digital assets, B2H embeds compassion, transparency, and long-term responsibility into its design.

Our Purpose

  • Embed compassion into blockchain technology
  • Support verified humanitarian initiatives and aid for the poor
  • Maintain transparency, accountability, and long-term trust

Our Values

  • Integrity over speed
  • Trust over hype
  • Patience over pressure
  • Humanity over profit

Who We Serve

Individuals who want to participate consciously, charities, NGOs, institutions, and communities in need who benefit from direct, responsible support.

Our Commitment

B2H is a humanitarian-first project. There are no guaranteed returns, no financial promises, and no shortcuts. Participation is voluntary, and the focus is long-term impact, not short-term speculation.

Founder’s Message

My name is Mubashir Munnawar.
B2H (Burn to Help | Born for Humanity) was born from a simple belief: that humanity can use technology responsibly to serve people in need.

This project is built on patience, transparency, and long-term responsibility. There are no guaranteed returns, no hidden agendas, and no rush. Participation, if any, is voluntary and based on shared values—not promises.

I am not a wealthy person, but I believe sincerity, accountability, and faith in doing the right thing matter more than capital. My commitment is to protect the integrity of this project for as long as I am able.

B2H exists to explore a new idea: that technology can be structured to help humanity while remaining ethical, lawful, and transparent.

— Founder, B2H
