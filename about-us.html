About Us

Our Story
B2H (Burn to Help | Born for Humanity) was created to explore a new way for technology to serve humanity. Unlike speculative digital assets, B2H was designed from the start to connect participation with real-world humanitarian impact.

Our Purpose
- Embed compassion into blockchain technology
- Support verified humanitarian initiatives and aid for the poor
- Maintain transparency, accountability, and long-term trust

How We Work
- Fixed Supply Token: 500 billion B2H tokens, no future minting
- Burn-to-Help Mechanism: Every defined burn event contributes to charitable allocations and reduces circulating supply
- Separate Funds: Charity, operational, and treasury funds are managed transparently
- Founder Stewardship: Project guided ethically, not for personal gain

Our Values
Integrity over speed
Trust over hype
Patience over pressure
Humanity over profit

Who We Serve
Individuals who want to participate consciously
Charities, NGOs, and institutions seeking transparency
Communities in need who benefit from direct, responsible support

Our Commitment
B2H is a humanitarian-first project. There are no guaranteed returns, no financial promises, and no shortcuts. Participation is voluntary, and the focus is long-term impact, not short-term speculation.

Our goal is to build a project that is respected globally, trusted by humans and institutions, and moral by design — a legacy that can continue beyond the founder.
