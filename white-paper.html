Overview

B2H (Burn to Help | Born for Humanity) transforms participation into lasting humanitarian benefit.

Built on patience, transparency, ethical restraint, and long-term responsibility, B2H demonstrates that technology can protect dignity and deliver sustainable impact.

Purpose & Core Values

  • Embed compassion and responsibility into technology
  • Support verified humanitarian initiatives for the poor and vulnerable
  • Build long-term systems that outlive individuals
  • Maintain ethical transparency without causing harm

Core Values: Integrity over speed · Trust over hype · Patience over pressure · Humanity over profit

Token Overview

  • Name: B2H
  • Total Supply: 500,000,000,000 B2H
  • Supply Type: Fixed — no inflation, no future minting

Fixed supply ensures fairness, discipline, and protection from dilution.

Burn-to-Help Mechanism

B2H burn events permanently reduce circulating supply while activating humanitarian allocations. Burns align participation with responsibility, long-term impact, and sustainability — not speculation.

Transparency & Ethics

  • No promised profits or financial returns
  • No price targets or projections
  • Clear separation between charity, operations, and treasury
  • Founder stewardship without ownership or personal gain

Vision & Mission

Vision: Systems that deliver lasting relief and opportunity, regardless of wealth or location.

Mission: Convert contributions into sustainable assets continuously serving humanity, creating true Sadaqah Jariyah.

Ethical Framework

  • Transparency in all operations
  • No personal gain for founders or trustees
  • Faith-aligned financial practices (no Riba)
  • Continuous oversight by rotating ethical council

Long-Term Impact

B2H contributions become permanent assets generating continuous support for vulnerable communities.

How to Participate

Participation is voluntary, transparent, and open to all. Contributors create a living system of good, impacting generations.

Our Commitment

B2H is a humanitarian instrument. We uphold long-term stewardship, transparency, and ethical management, ensuring technology serves humanity with integrity and compassion.
